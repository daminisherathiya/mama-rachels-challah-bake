This opportunity is thousands of years in the making!

For thousands of years, Klal Yisrael has been davening at Kever Rachel, beseeching our mother to intercede for her children in galus. 

For thousands of years, women have been davening during the time of hafrashas challah, one of women’s special mitzvos, for everything they need for themselves and their families.

And now, for the first time, these special opportunities will converge.

Join thousands of women worldwide at Mama Rachel's Challah Bake, a celebration of tefillah and connection livestreamed from one of the holiest sites in the world – Kever Rachel.

Send in your name and request, and your kvittel will be read aloud during the upcoming challah bake at Kever Rachel. Your support will fund Mosdos Kever Rachel’s many kollelim at the site, harnessing the power of limud HaTorah, as well as tefillah during hafrashas challah.
